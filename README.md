# Value Engineering Journal

A public record of the thinking behind Value Engineering — a proposed new branch
of computer science concerned with how machines represent, preserve, and reason
about *value*, not just data.

Written in Nairobi, Kenya.

## About this series

Computer science has spent seventy years getting very good at moving data
around — numbers, records, documents, language. It has never developed a
native way to represent *value*: what something is worth, to whom, in what
context, and at what cost to preserve.

This journal is where that gap gets worked through in public, one entry at a
time. Entry 000 introduces the discipline itself, and **Daturrency**, its
first working theory — a model for turning passive digital money into an
active, goal-aware knowledge object.
